Answers

  1. Why are template areas easy to understand?
    They use readable names instead of numbers, looks like actual layout.
  2. Which section spans full width?
    Header and Footer span full width (both columns).
  3. Can we rearrange layout by changing area names?
    Yes, just change the template-areas order to rearrange.
  4. Is this better than column numbers?
    Yes, easier to read and maintain for complex layouts.
  5. Where are template areas mostly used?
    Page layouts, dashboards, complex website structures.
