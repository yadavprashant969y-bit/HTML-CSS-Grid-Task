Answers

  1. What are grid lines?
    Invisible lines that divide the grid. 3 columns have 4 lines (1, 2, 3, 4).
  2. How many columns does the item span?
    2 columns (from line 1 to line 3).
  3. What happens to other items?
    They shift to fill remaining space automatically.
  4. Can two items overlap?
    Yes, if you manually position them on same cells.
  5. Where is column spanning used in websites?
    Headers, banners, featured content, full-width sections.
