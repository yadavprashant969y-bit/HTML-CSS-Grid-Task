Answers

  1. What does fr stand for?
    fr stands for "fraction" - it represents a fraction of available space.
  2. How many columns are created?
    3 columns are created (1fr 1fr 1fr).
  3. What happens when screen size is reduced?
    Columns shrink proportionally, each stays equal in size.
  4. Do items overflow or adjust?
    Items adjust - they shrink with their columns.
  5. Why is fr better than px for layouts?
    fr is flexible and responsive, px is fixed and can cause overflow.
